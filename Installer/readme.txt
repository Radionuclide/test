Installation:
Have ibaAnalyzer installed and registered as a COM-component, the version of ibaAnalyzer should be at least 5.0
Also install the 2.0 framework, available form www.microsoft.com and
	http://www.iba-ag.com/pub/ftp/ibaGent/2.0framework/dotnetfx.exe
Execute ibaDatCoordinaterInstall_v1.5.exe and follow instructions.
