Installation:
Have ibaAnalyzer installed and registered as a COM-component, the version of ibaAnalyzer should be at least 5.0 and to enable conditional tasks, the ibaAnalyzer version should be at least 5.4
Also install the 2.0 framework, available form www.microsoft.com and
	http://www.iba-ag.com/pub/ftp/ibaGent/2.0framework/dotnetfx.exe
Execute ibaDatCoordinaterInstall_v1.5.exe and follow instructions.
